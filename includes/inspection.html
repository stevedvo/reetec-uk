Inspection & Testing Services

Reetec Electrical Services offers a full range of inspection and testing services on both fixed and portable equipment for domestic, commercial and industrial installations.

All installations will deteriorate with age and legislation requires that all electrical installations are maintained in a safe condition and therefore must be periodically inspected. Properties should be inspected at the change of occupancy or within a set yearly period for the type of property.

We recognise the importance of testing and inspection, and have invested in the latest test equipment and computer software to ensure these services are carried out to the highest standard.

Testing is carried out using modern regularly calibrated test equipment. Results are logged onto a computer database where NICEIC reports and certificates can be saved for future reference and then printed off and issued to the client who ordered the initial works.

Whether you require a periodic inspection and test to comply with an insurance policy, mortgage application, or for the investigation of potential or known problems within your installation, we have the equipment, and know how to give you the answers you need.

  • Electrical Installation Certificates
  • Domestic Electrical Installation Certificates
  • Periodic Inspection & Testing Reports
  • Minor Works Certificates
  • Emergency Lighting Certificates

Before being put into service all new electrical installations will be inspected and tested to verify, that as far as is reasonably practicable, that the requirements of the regulations have been met. Upon completion of works, an electrical installation certificate or a minor works certificate will be issued.

All our periodic inspections & testing is carried out to BS7671 (IEE Regulations) in accordance with current legal requirements. A detailed report will be provided highlighting any problems and the remedial work required.
