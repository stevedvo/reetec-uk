Domestic Installation Services

Reetec Electrical Services are affiliated members of the NICEIC as Part P registered domestic installers. All work carried out by the company complies with current British Standards meeting current Part P legislation. The company caters for all aspects of electrical work from just an extra plug socket or light fitting in your home to new build developments to the more specialist lighting and security systems in large properties.

Reetec Electrical services can plan and specify the most appropriate value for money solution that satisfies your vision. We understand home improvement can be a difficult and disruptive time and therefore don't want to add any unnecessary stress to the process. We are committed to providing efficient and effective quality solutions with the minimum disruption to you and your home environment.

From additional sockets to complete rewires, Reetec Electrical Services can take care of all your electrical requirements.

  • Full and partial re-wiring
  • New builds and housing developments
  • Extensions and conversions
  • Consumer unit upgrade
  • Earthing to main services upgrade
  • Electric showers
  • Storage heating
  • Supply and installation of modern low voltage down lighting
  • External flood lighting
  • Garden lighting and power
  • Data cabling and computing solutions
  • Smoke and fire alarm systems
  • Intruder alarms
  • Supplies for new equipment including hot tubs
  • CCTV
  • Video door entry
  • Automated gate controls and garage door openers
